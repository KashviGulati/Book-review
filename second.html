The diary of a young girl

Genre: Autobiography

Review: This book is the personal journal of Anne Frank, a jewish girl who was one of the witnesses during the holocaust with her family. It takes us through the horrors of world war 2 and concentration camps and people who lost everything during the war. It stands as a powerful testament to the impact of war on an individual and remains an important historical document
